Our Platform!
As a student-led party, Hope revises its platform every year at the Spring GBM. However, we have four primary points as to which we lay as a foundation for the rest of our work.

1. Transparency. All of our meetings, candidate policies, bill information, and developments will be made public. We will also require that every student government funded agency, club, or group make its full budget public.

2. Accountability. Student Government should be for the students, not the other way around. To that extent, we will lower the requirements for a recall election, and we will require that all student government officials be subject to recall elections at the request of 200 students.

3. Student Services. We will work to improve student services and resources, and cut those that are not effective or cost more than they are worth to the students. We will also work to make these same services more democratic, by allowing students to be elected to committees for these services.

4. Advocacy. Currently, Student Government is failing to stand up for its students. We will fix that by developing student government committees for local and state advocacy, as well as holding university admin subject to the students.
