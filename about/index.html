WHAT IS HOPE PARTY?
We are a new UF Student Government party established by concerned students for the purpose of developing a more open Student Government at the University of Florida, and to bring perspectives of students who are not represented by the current one-party system under Vision Party. Following the collapse of Change Party and Watch Party, Vision now stands unopposed in leading the UF Student Government, but we hope to work as a voice for the student body as a whole.
